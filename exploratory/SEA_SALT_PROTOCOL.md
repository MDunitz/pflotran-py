## Experimental Protocol

1. Pour salt water into the pitcher
1. Half fill the glass tray with sea water from the pitcher
1. Put the glass tray into the lab oven
1. Fill the tray the rest of the way with salt water, almost to the top, be careful not to splash any water into the oven
1. Cover any excess saltwater in the pitcher with parafilm and store on Madison's bench
1. Heat at ~90°C for ~48 hours (until fully crystallized and all water has evaporated)
1. Scrape the salt with the spatula and scoop out the contents with the spoon
1. Weigh the salt, cover with aluminum foil, label with date and weight and leave on Madison's bench
